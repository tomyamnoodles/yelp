TOUCHY TERRORS

Touchy Terrors is a yucky, icky project that aims to investigate the connections between visual imagery and fear responses associated with texture aversion. We utilise everyday materials to conduct research create our artefacts.


A project by Arena, Jessica, Khalisa, Yoonjin
